			MathCards Rules
1. The player has two tables: attack and defense, similarly to the opponent
2. The player can place cards on his tables (attack and defense)
   with mathematical symbols or numbers
3. The cards are placed on the table in Reverse Polish Notation (RPN)
4. The damage dealt to the opponent is the difference between the value
   on the player's attack table and the opponent's defense table
5. The player can place any cards on their table, while on the opponent's table
   only symbols
6. If the equation on the opponent's defense table is not written in RPN,
   the opponent deals double damage
7. The game ends when both players run out of cards, and the player with
   the most health or the one who reduces the opponent's health to 0 wins
   or by most deafult when 10 round hits and player with most hp wins.
